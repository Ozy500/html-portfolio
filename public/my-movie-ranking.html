The Best Movies According to Oziel

My top 3 movies of all-time

The Lord of The Rings Trilogy

These are my favorite movies of all time.
They are obviously a trilogy but I count them as one because the whole story cannot be told in just one movie.
I love this fantasy and these movies, in my opinion, are some of the best.

Kill Bill Vol. 1 & Vol. 2

Again, these movies are counted as one for the same reason.
I've always loved a good action movie and these perfectly fit the bill. (Get it? Fit the "bill"?)
Although, in full disclosure, these movies are not for the faint of heart.
I say this because these movies are very gory.

Cowboy Bebop

Now, this one is an anime. "Japanese animation."
I love this one because of the action as well.
It does include a bit of a love story, so that adds a bit of emotion to it.
